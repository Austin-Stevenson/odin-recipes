Ultimate Ice Cream Sandwich

Description

A dense decadent cookie ice cream sandwich all from scratch.

Ingredients

Cookie Base:

  • Water
  • 2 cups (315 g) semi-sweet chocolate
  • 1 cup (227 g) salted butter
  • 1 cup (210 g) granulated sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Egg yolk
  • 2 tsp (6 g) vanilla extract
  • 2 cups (300 g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp (5 g) baking soda
  • 2 tsp (5 g) espresso powder
  • 1/2 cup (60 g) unsweetened cocoa powder

Ice Cream:

  • 3 cups (700 ml) whole milk
  • 1 vanilla bean pod
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 1.5 cups (260 g) granulated white sugar
  • 3 cups (700 ml) heavy whipping cream

Cookie:

  • 3/4 cup (168 g) unsalted butter
  • 3/4 cup (161 g) packed light brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup (150 g) granulated white sugar
  • 1.5 tsp (9 g) sea salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 1/4 cup (197 g) all-purpose flour
  • 8 oz (225 g) 60-70% cocoa dark chocolate, roughly chopped
  • Flaky salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Fill a medium saucepan with 1-inch of water. Bring to a boil before reducing to a simmer.
  3. Place a heatproof bowl over the simmering water, and add in the chocolate and salted butter. Heat and melt while stirring occasionally until completely melted.
  4. Remove from heat and whisk in granulated sugar, eggs (1 at a time), egg yolk, and vanilla extract.
  5. In a separate bowl, combine all-purpose flour, baking soda, espresso powder, unsweetened cocoa powder and whisk together.
  6. While whisking, slowly pour in the chocolate mixture into the flour and whisk until combined.
  7. Spread the chocolate mixture evenly among the baking sheets and press down to fill the entire sheet.
  8. Bake in a 350F oven for 10-12 minutes. Remove and cool for 12-15 minutes on a wire rack. Place in a freezer for at least 15 minutes or up to 2 hours.
  9. Spread the ice cream mixture on top of one the chocolate cookie base. Take the second cookie base and invert it onto the ice cream.
  10. Press down firmly and place the other baking sheet on top. Place in the freezer for 4 hours or overnight.
  11. Remove the parchment paper and cut it into rectangular shapes. Move quickly before it melts.
  12. Wrap each piece in plastic wrap and freeze until ready to serve.
